01 — About

Hi, I'm Shreya!

I'm someone who loves exploring new places, learning interesting facts, and finding creativity in everyday things. Whether it's travelling to a new destination or making unique home décor through arts and crafts, I enjoy experiences that let me learn and create.

I'm naturally curious and love understanding how things work, which is one of the reasons I became interested in digital marketing. I enjoy observing trends, seeing what catches people's attention, and learning how ideas can reach the right audience.

I believe there's always something new to learn, and I'm excited to keep growing, exploring new ideas, and creating meaningful work along the way.

02 — Off duty

What I do with my time

Not a job title in sight — just the things that keep me curious.

01

Arts & Crafts

I like taking things apart, literally — leftover fabric, old jars, spare wood — and turning them into decor that has a story attached to it. It's the same instinct as content, honestly: start with raw material, shape it into something someone wants to keep looking at.

02

Travelling

New places, for me, are less about the postcard view and more about what happened there — the history, the local stories, the odd fact a guide mentions once and never repeats. I keep a running list of places I want to understand, not just visit.

03

Content Creation

Shooting, editing, captioning — figuring out how to make a place, a project, or a passing thought worth stopping to look at. It's the same curiosity as travelling and crafting, just pointed at a camera instead.

"The best trips and the best projects have the same secret — pay attention to the detail everyone else walks past."
