01 — About

Curious by nature.
Hands always busy.

I'm someone who's endlessly curious — whether that's exploring a new city and digging into its history and quirky little facts, or spending a weekend turning random materials into a piece of home décor nobody's seen before. I love making things, and I love understanding how things work.

That same curiosity is what pulled me toward digital marketing too — writing that connects, data that explains why, trends worth paying attention to. But this space isn't about that. This is just the things I'd talk about over coffee.

02 — Off duty

What I do with my time

Not a job title in sight — just the things that keep me curious.

01

Arts & Crafts

I like taking things apart, literally — leftover fabric, old jars, spare wood — and turning them into decor that has a story attached to it. It's the same instinct as content, honestly: start with raw material, shape it into something someone wants to keep looking at.

02

Travelling

New places, for me, are less about the postcard view and more about what happened there — the history, the local stories, the odd fact a guide mentions once and never repeats. I keep a running list of places I want to understand, not just visit.

03

Reading

Usually whatever's within reach — fiction to slow down, non-fiction to feed the same curiosity that shows up everywhere else in my life. Half-read books on the nightstand are a permanent feature.

"The best trips and the best projects have the same secret — pay attention to the detail everyone else walks past."
